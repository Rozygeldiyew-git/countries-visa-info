Visa requirements NOTE: We recommend using Adobe Acrobat XI software to fill out the electronic visa application! The main documents which are necessary to apply for a visa to Turkmenistan by Consular Section of the Embassy of Turkmenistan in Turkey: Valid national passport. Invitation approved by the State Immigration Service of Turkmenistan. 1 filled Visa application form. 1 colored photos of the applicant (3x4cm). The Certificate of health examination for international traveller. Citizens who is visiting Turkmenistan for the period of 3 months or more, have to present the certificate of AIDS-test Children are exempt from visa fee and children between 7-16 years old are subject to 50 % discount. Visa will be processed during 3-5 days after the presentation of documents. Urgent visas will be processed during a day after the presentation of documents. Foreign citizens and stateless persons (hereinafter referred to as the foreign citizens) may be issued the following categories of visas for entry into Turkmenistan and exit from Turkmenistan (hereinafter â€“ visas): 1) Diplomatic visa (DP); 2) Official visa (OF); 3) Business visa (BS1, BS2); 4) Investment visa (IN); 5) Work visa (WP); 6) Family visa (FM); 7) Sports visa (SP1, SP2); 8) Humanitarian visa (HM); 9) Private visa (PR1, PR2); 10) Student visa (ST); 11) Tourist visa (TU); 12) Transit visa (TR1, TR2); 13) Health visa (HL); 14) Driver's visa (DR); 15) Exit visa (EX). Relevant documents for obtaining work permits in Turkmenistan (hereinafter - work permits) and work visas for foreign citizens are subject to the requirement together. For visas of each category are required: a written or electronic application of the established sample and the attached documents, including color photographs of size 5*6 of the established sample, 1 copy of the Personal file for issuance of Business (BS1, BS2), investment (IN), working (WP), family (FM), sports (SP1, SP2), humanitarian (HM), private (PR1, PR2), student (ST), tourist (TU), transit (TR1, TR2), health (HL), driver's visa (DR) to foreign citizens. For obtaining diplomatic (DP), service (OF) and exit (EX) visas to foreign citizens arriving to Turkmenistan, as well as to persons arriving to Turkmenistan with the purpose of visiting seriously ill relatives, burial of close relatives and carrying out burial ceremonies, humanitarian and other urgent character, and also various visas of the established sample in cases of the stay under the necessity of foreign citizens in Turkmenistan for obtaining business (BS1, BS2), investment (IN), working (WP), family (FM), sports (SP1, SP2), humanitarian (HM), private (PR1, PR2), student (ST), tourist (TU), transit (TR1, TR2), health (HL), driver's visa (DR) to foreign citizens Personal file is not required.
